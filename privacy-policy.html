Mude Circle
Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 8, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Mude Circle collects and processes the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address and authentication credentials
  • Annonymous Mood Data: Annonymous patterns such as 'user logged a mood' (we never track the content of your moods or notes)
  • Usage Data: App interactions and analytics (via Mixpanel)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Mude Circle service
  • Enable mood tracking and sharing features
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Send important service notifications
  • Process subscription payments (via RevenueCat)

3. Data Sharing

Your mood data is only shared with:

  • Circle Members: Only users you explicitly invite to your circles
  • Service Providers: Supabase (hosting), Mixpanel (analytics), RevenueCat (payments)

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Your Privacy Controls

You have control over your data:

  • Choose which moods to share or keep private
  • Manage who is in your circles and what circles you are a part of
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time
  • Request a copy of your data

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Mude Circle is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you're in the EU/UK, you have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability

9. Analytics

We use Mixpanel for analytics with IP anonymization enabled to comply with GDPR.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.

11. Contact Us

For privacy questions, data requests or account deletion, contact us at: hello@mudecircle.com
